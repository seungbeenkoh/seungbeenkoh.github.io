About

I am an undergraduate student in Biochemistry at Yonsei University with research experience in experimental protein structural biochemistry, especially GPCR and receptor-ligand systems. My work has centered on connecting molecular structure to biochemical function and therapeutic relevance. Through projects involving UR2R/hU2 complex preparation, somatostatin analog structural analysis, SSTR5 purification support, ASH1-related biochemical validation, and chromatin biology workflows, I have developed a broad experimental foundation for studying proteins and protein-targeting molecules.

GPCR structural biochemistry

Human UR2R/hU2 complex expression, purification, and preliminary cryo-EM analysis

Established an experimental workflow for producing ligand-bound human urotensin 2 receptor and preparing the active UR2R(11-336)-hU2-mGqi-scFv16 signaling complex for preliminary cryo-EM characterization. This work resulted in a first-author publication in Protein Expression and Purification.
